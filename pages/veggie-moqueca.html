Vegetarian Moqueca with Banana

A Brazilian classic made with plantains, banana, coconut milk and dendê oil.

Vegetarian
45 min 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 plantains, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 medium red onion, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp dendê oil (palm oil)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 hot pepper, seeds removed, finely chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • White rice to serve

Instructions

  1. Slice the plantains and marinate with lime juice and salt for 10 minutes
  2. Meanwhile, dice the bell peppers, tomatoes and onion, mince the garlic and chop the cilantro
  3. Heat the dendê oil and olive oil in a large pan over medium heat
  4. Sauté the onion until soft, then add the garlic and cook until golden
  5. Add the bell peppers and tomatoes and cook for 3 minutes
  6. Arrange the plantain slices in a layer over the vegetables
  7. Pour in the coconut milk, add the hot pepper and cover with a lid
  8. Cook on low heat for 15 minutes until the broth thickens
  9. Finish with fresh cilantro and serve with white rice
