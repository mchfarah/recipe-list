Tomato Soup

A rich and velvety soup, perfect for a cozy evening.

Soup
30 min 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated parmesan to serve

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat
  2. Add the onion and cook until soft and golden, about 8 minutes
  3. Add the garlic and cook for 1 more minute until fragrant
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth
  5. Add the sugar, salt and pepper and stir well
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes
  7. Add the fresh basil and blend the soup until smooth and creamy
  8. Stir in the heavy cream and heat for 2 more minutes
  9. Serve hot, topped with grated parmesan and fresh basil leaves
