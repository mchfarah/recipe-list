Potato and Onion Soup

A warm and creamy soup, perfect for cold days and getting rid of the flu.

Soup
45 min 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 medium onions, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1L vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley to garnish
  • Grated parmesan to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat
  2. Add the onions and cook until soft and golden, about 8 minutes
  3. Add the garlic and cook for 1 more minute until fragrant
  4. Add the diced potatoes and stir well
  5. Pour in the vegetable broth and season with salt and pepper
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 25 minutes until potatoes are tender
  7. Blend the soup until smooth and creamy
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley and grated parmesan
