Caprese Avocado Toast

Crispy bread topped with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes and basil.

Vegetarian
10 min 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 thick slices of sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 150g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread in a skillet with olive oil for about 2 minutes on each side until golden
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit and scoop the flesh into a bowl
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork, season with salt and pepper
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto each toast
  5. Top with fresh mozzarella slices and cherry tomatoes
  6. Garnish with fresh basil leaves
  7. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and serve immediately
