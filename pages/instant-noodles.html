Instant Noodles turma do Chico Bento.

A quick Brazilian classic, the best for hangovers and lazy days.

Instant Noodles
3 min 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Chico Bento instant noodles (chicken and tomato flavor)
  • 500ml water
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Chopped green onions to garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a small pan
  2. Add the noodles and cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Turn off the heat and add the seasoning packet
  4. Stir well until the seasoning is fully dissolved
  5. If desired, add a fried or poached egg on top
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve immediately
