Chocolate Brownie

Rich, fudgy and impossible to resist, even better with ice cream!

Dessert
40 min 12 servings

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate chips
  • 170g unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Vanilla ice cream to serve

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C and line a square baking pan with parchment paper
  2. Melt the butter and chocolate chips together in a microwave in 30-second bursts, stirring between each
  3. Whisk in the granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, whisking well after each
  5. Fold in the flour, cocoa powder and salt until just combined — do not overmix
  6. Pour the batter into the pan and spread evenly
  7. Bake for 25 minutes — the center should look slightly underdone
  8. Let cool for 30 minutes before slicing
  9. Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream
