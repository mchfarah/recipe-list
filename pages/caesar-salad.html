Caesar Salad

Fresh and crunchy, perfect for a light lunch.

Salad
15 min 2 servings

Ingredients

  • Salad
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • Homemade Croutons
  • 2 slices crusty bread, torn into pieces
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Caesar Dressing
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1 tsp anchovy paste
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F)
  2. Toss the bread pieces with olive oil and salt, spread on a baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes until golden and crispy
  3. Meanwhile, whisk together all dressing ingredients in a small bowl until smooth
  4. Wash, dry and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces
  5. Place the lettuce in a large bowl and drizzle with the dressing, tossing well to coat
  6. Add the parmesan and croutons and toss again
  7. Serve immediately with extra parmesan on top
