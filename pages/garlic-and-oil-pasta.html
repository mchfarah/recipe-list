Garlic and Oil Pasta

A classic, simple and always delicious!

Pasta
20 min 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley to garnish
  • Grated parmesan to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions
  2. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat
  3. Add the onion and cook until soft, about 3 minutes
  4. Add the garlic slices and cook until golden, about 2 minutes
  5. Add the red pepper flakes and stir
  6. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan
  7. Toss well and garnish with fresh parsley
  8. Serve and top with grated parmesan
