Greek Salad

A refreshing mix of tomatoes, olives and feta cheese.

Salad
10 min 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe tomatoes, cut into chunks
  • 1 cucumber, sliced into half moons
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced into rings
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 150g feta cheese, cut into cubes
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion and bell pepper and place in a large bowl
  2. Add the Kalamata olives and season with salt and oregano
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar and toss gently
  4. Top with the feta cheese cubes — do not mix, leave them on top
  5. Sprinkle a final pinch of oregano over the feta
  6. Let the salad rest for 15 minutes before serving so the flavors blend together
